4. Understanding your results

Overall score

Your overall score (0-100) reflects the general health of your business. It's calculated as the average of the 6 pillars.

How to interpret your overall score:

  • 70-100 (Green): Your business is in excellent health. Focus on optimization and innovation.
  • 40-69 (Amber): Your business has good foundations but needs improvements. Identify priorities.
  • 0-39 (Red): Your business requires immediate attention. Plan urgent corrective actions.
